Our Story

Preserving Andhra's pickle traditions since 2010

Traditional Roots

Founded in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, Spicy Delights began as a family kitchen preserving recipes passed down through generations. Our methods remain unchanged - copper vessels, sun-drying, and 45-day natural fermentation.

Authentic Ingredients

We use only the finest local produce: Guntur's fiery red chilies, Chittoor's tangy mangoes, and Kurnool's cold-pressed sesame oil. No artificial preservatives - just pure, natural flavors.

Crafted with Care

Each batch is prepared with the same love and attention our grandmothers put into their pickles. We don't just make food - we preserve memories and traditions.
